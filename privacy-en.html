Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

1. Who are we?

PicEvent is a mobile app for sharing photos and videos between participants of the same event (wedding, birthday, trip, corporate event…). The publisher of PicEvent is the data controller.

Contact: Piceventcontacte@gmail.com

2. What data do we collect?

  • Profile: the first name (or nickname) you enter, an automatically generated username, an avatar colour or photo if you choose one, and the account creation date.
  • Technical identifier: an anonymous identifier provided by Firebase Authentication. No email address, phone number or password is required to use the app.
  • Content: the photos, videos, comments and likes you post in albums, and the list of albums you create or join.
  • Preferences: your chosen language (French/English), stored only on your device.
  • Purchases: any transactions (album creation) are processed by Google Play or the App Store. We never receive or store your payment details.

3. Why do we process this data?

Solely to operate the service: creating and joining albums, showing your content to the other members of the album, and managing your account. PicEvent shows no ads, never sells your data and does not use it for profiling.

Legal basis: performance of the contract (providing the service) and your consent, given when creating your account.

4. Who can see your content?

Photos, videos and comments posted in an album are only visible to people who have that album's invitation code. They are not public.

5. Where is your data hosted?

  • Google Firebase (authentication and database);
  • Cloudinary (photo and video storage).

These providers may process data outside the European Union; such transfers are covered by appropriate safeguards (the European Commission's standard contractual clauses).

6. How long do we keep your data?

  • Every album automatically expires 5 months after its creation: its photos, videos and comments are then permanently deleted, including from the media storage servers.
  • Your profile is kept for as long as your account exists. You can delete it at any time (see below).

7. Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict and object to the processing of your data, as well as a right to data portability.

  • Access and portability: the "My data" section of the app lets you view and export your data in JSON format.
  • Erasure: account deletion, available in the app, genuinely removes your profile, photos, videos, comments and likes, from both our database and the media storage.
  • For any other request: Piceventcontacte@gmail.com.

You may also lodge a complaint with your data protection authority — in France, the CNIL (www.cnil.fr).

8. Minimum age

In accordance with French law, PicEvent may only be used by people aged 15 or over, or under 15 with the consent of a parent or legal guardian. This condition is accepted when creating the account.

9. Security

Communications with our servers are encrypted (HTTPS) and access to data is restricted by strict security rules. As no system is infallible, please only post content you are comfortable sharing with the members of your albums.

10. Changes

This policy may be updated; the date at the top of the page indicates the latest version. You will be informed in the app of any significant change.
